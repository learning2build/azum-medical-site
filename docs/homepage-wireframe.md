# AZUM Medical — Homepage Wireframe Blueprint

## Overall Design Direction

### Brand Feel
Modern concierge healthcare combined with a welcoming boutique clinic experience.

The site should feel:

- calm  
- premium  
- human  
- wellness-forward  
- trustworthy  

Avoid a corporate hospital aesthetic.

---

### Primary Palette

**Base**
- Soft white / ivory

**Primary Brand**
- Purple from logo

**Accent**
- Gold (light use only)

**Secondary Neutrals**
- Soft gray  
- Muted lavender tones  

---

### Typography

**Headlines**
- Elegant serif or refined modern serif  
- Conveys trust and premium healthcare

**Body**
- Clean sans-serif  
- Highly readable medical style

---

### Spacing

Layout should feel **airy and spacious**.

- Large vertical spacing
- Minimal heavy containers
- Avoid dense sections

Use:

- cards
- layered sections
- subtle shadows

Avoid thick borders and box-heavy layouts.

---
